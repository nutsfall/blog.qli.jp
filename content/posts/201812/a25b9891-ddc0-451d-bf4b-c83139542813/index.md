[Things 3.7 Brings Siri Shortcuts and More - Things Blog - Cultured Code](https://culturedcode.com/things/blog/2018/09/things-3-7-brings-siri-shortcuts-and-more/)

[OmniFocus 3.1 for Mac: Light Sidebar, Localizations, and More - The Omni Group](https://www.omnigroup.com/blog/omnifocus-3.1-for-mac)
